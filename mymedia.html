The OR command in SQL displays a record if any of the conditions separated by OR is TRUE. If any of the conditions it connects to are true, the OR operator returns true. 
                    SELECT column1, column2, ...
                    FROM table_name
                    WHERE condition1 OR condition2 OR condition3 ...;
