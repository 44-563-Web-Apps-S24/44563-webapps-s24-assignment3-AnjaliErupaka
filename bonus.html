Elsa Auburn the Elephant

Introducing Elsa Auburn, a magnificent elephant that exudes warmth and grace with her gorgeous auburn coat. Elsa is a well-liked character in the made-up world that she lives in because of her reputation for kindness and compassion. With her ivory tusks, Elsa towers over others and captivates them with her majestic demeanor.

Games Played by Elsa Auburn:

  • Gentle Trunk Tag
  • Ivory Insights
  • Safari Soiree
